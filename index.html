About Me

Hello! I'm Dragan, a Unity Developer passionate about creating polished, performant, and scalable applications. My journey started back in 2014, and since then I've had the privilege of working on everything from mobile games and educational prototypes to VR cleanroom simulations and city-scale digital twins.

I specialize in taking unstable codebases and transforming them into clean, testable systems using principles like SOLID, MVC, and efficient dependency injection with VContainer/Zenject.
